Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olives
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice

Preparation Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix vegetables in a bowl.
  3. Add olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss well and serve fresh.
