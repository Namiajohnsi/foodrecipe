Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken / Vegetables
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Biryani spices
  • Ginger & garlic paste

Preparation Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken/vegetables with yogurt and spices.
  3. Cook rice until 70% done.
  4. Layer rice and masala in a pot.
  5. Cook on low heat (dum) for 20 minutes.
